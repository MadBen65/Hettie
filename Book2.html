SKANDAR & THE PHANTOM RIDER

Skandar is a book about a boy named Skandar who embarks on an epic adventure with bloodthirsty unicorns. He discovers that the Silver circle are killing wild unicorns making the magic of the island unbalanced which is causing possessions. He faces this journey with his quartet: Bobby Bruna, Mitchell Henderson, and Florence Shekoni (a silver), along with his unicorn, Scoundrel's Luck.
