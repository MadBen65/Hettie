Skandar Book 1

Skandar is a book about a boy named Skandar who embarks on an epic adventure with bloodthirsty unicorns. He discovers that he is allied to an illegal element. He faces this journey with his quartet: Bobby Bruna, Mitchell Henderson, and Florence Shekoni, along with his unicorn, Scoundrel's Luck.
