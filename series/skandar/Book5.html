SKANDAR & THE SPIRIT WAR

Skandar is about a boy named Skandar Smith who embarks on an epic journey with blood thirsty unicorns, in this final book Skandar faces an epic war against the silvers and the non-spying sentinels he faces this war with almost 90% of the island's population.
