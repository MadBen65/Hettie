A.F. Steadman

A.F Steadman is a creative and talented author known for her world-famous children's series 'Skandar'. Annabelle Steadman was originally a professional lawyer before she pursued her Master's degree in Creative Writing at Cambridge.

Author Facts

  • Her books have been translated into 47 languages worldwide
  • She has been awarded the Waterstones Children's Book Prize and the Branford Boase Award
  • She has a degree in law and worked as a lawyer before becoming a full-time author
  • She has a Master's degree in Creative Writing from Cambridge University
  • She is known for her world-famous children's series 'Skandar'
