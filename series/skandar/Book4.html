SKANDAR & THE SKELETON CURSE

Skandar is a book about a boy named Skandar who embarks on an epic adventure with bloodthirsty unicorns. He has to fix the skeleton curse that his older sister Kenna inflicted upon the island ,which means all the bonded unicorns turned wild ones bloodthirsty and rotting ,everything. He faces this journey with his quartet: Bobby Bruna, Mitchell Henderson, and Florence Shekoni and of course his unicorn but also partially his aunt Agatha Everheart
