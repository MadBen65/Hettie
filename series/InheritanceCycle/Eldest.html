Eldest (2005)

Eldest is the second book in the Inheritance Cycle series by Christopher Paolini. It continues the story of Eragon, a teenage Dragon Rider, as he faces new challenges and adventures in his quest to defeat the evil king Galbatorix. The book delves deeper into the lore of Alagaësia, exploring themes of friendship, loyalty, and the struggle between good and evil. Whilst he trains with the elves to become a more powerful Dragon Rider, with the only other dragon rider on his side training him, he gets stronger and stronger, but he also faces new enemies.
