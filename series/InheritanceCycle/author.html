Christopher Paolini

Christopher Paolini is the author of The Inheritance Cycle, a young-adult high-fantasy saga set in Alagaesia.

Quick facts

  • Author of: Eragon, Eldest, Brisingr, Inheritance, Murtagh, and The Fork, the Witch, and the Worm
  • Core cycle years: 2002 - 2011
  • World of Eragon titles also include The Fork, the Witch, and the Worm and Murtagh
  • Born: November 17, 1983
