The Inheritance Cycle

Christopher Paolini's core Inheritance Cycle follows Eragon and Saphira in Alagaesia as they challenge Galbatorix and rebuild the Dragon Rider legacy.

Main books in order: Eragon (2003), Eldest (2005), Brisingr (2008), Inheritance (2011).
