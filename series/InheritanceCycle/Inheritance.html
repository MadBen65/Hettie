Inheritance (2011)

Inheritance is the fourth and final book in the Inheritance Cycle series by Christopher Paolini. It brings the cycle to its climax, with the rebellion's final push, major character resolutions, and a new future for Riders and dragons. Eragon searches for the Vault of Souls, where he finds the Eldunarí, the hearts of the dragons, which are the source of their power. He also learns that he is a Rider and that he has a dragon named Saphira. The book explores themes of destiny, sacrifice, and the consequences of choices made throughout the series.
