The First Unicorn Rider

This prequel to the Skandar series explores the story of George Penhaligon the first person to ever stumble upon the island and bond to a unicorn. His bonded unicorn's name is Rebel Spirit he also meets Rose and her silver unicorn Silver Shanty.
