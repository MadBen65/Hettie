SKANDAR & THE CHAOS TRIALS

Skandar is a book about a boy named Skandar who embarks on an epic adventure with bloodthirsty unicorns. He has to face the chaos trials and Scoundrel's Luck rebelling and with his sister Kenna not trusting him it's become a lot worse. He faces this journey with his quartet: Bobby Bruna, Mitchell Henderson, and Florence Shekoni.
